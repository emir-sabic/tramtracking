About Us

Welcome to the Sarajevo Tram Tracking system. This app was developed by a dedicated student for his final thesis. The idea for the app came after an unfortunate tram accident, which left the student waiting at a station without knowing the tram's status. This inspired the development of a real-time tram tracking system to help commuters stay informed and plan their journeys more effectively.

Our mission is to provide accurate and real-time information about tram locations, ensuring that you can navigate the city's tram system with confidence and ease. Whether you are a daily commuter or a visitor exploring Sarajevo, our app aims to enhance your travel experience by keeping you updated on tram movements.

We are constantly working to improve our service and expand our features. Your feedback and support are crucial in helping us achieve our goals.
